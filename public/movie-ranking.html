The Best movies according to Prashanth

My favourite 3 movies from 2024

Thalavan

This is a 2024 Indian Malayalam-language crime thriller film directed by Jis Joy and written by Anand Thevarkkat and Sarath Perumbavoor. The film stars Biju Menon and Asif Ali, with Miya George, Anusree, Dileesh Pothan and Kottayam Nazeer in supporting roles.

Golam

The dead body of Issac John, the MD of V-Tech Company, is found inside the restroom at his office with the door locked from the inside. The police is called in and the investigation begins. Initially it is presumed to be a case of accidental death from a head injury sustained during a slip and fall, and more apparently so with no signs of forced entry or confrontation on the body or the surroundings. However, his instincts cause investigator ASP Sandeep to suspect murder. John is also found to have been suffering from COPD. Police initially suspect Gibson George, John's business partner. force.

Aavesham

This is a 2024 Indian Malayalam-language action comedy film written and directed by Jithu Madhavan. The film is produced by Nazriya Nazim and Anwar Rasheed under Fahadh Faasil and Friends and Anwar Rasheed Entertainments. It stars Fahadh Faasil, Hipzster, Mithun Jai Shankar, Roshan Shanavas, Sajin Gopu, Midhutty and Mansoor Ali Khan. The film follows three youngsters in Bangalore who befriends a local gangster to help them take revenge on their school bully.
